ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

Sweet Gelato is a medium-sized business that strives to grow into an influential ice cream
company. Sweet Gelato is an ice cream business that has a variety of fruit-based flavors.
Flavors include chocolate, vanilla, orange, strawberry, blueberry, peach, pistachio, and
banana.This business sells ice cream that is of top-notch ingredients and can be
consistently supplied to its stores. Sweet Gelato started operating in June 2001. In an effort
to grow stronger and more effective in serving premium ingredients, Sweet Gelato
continually expands its company beyond expectations. Sweet Gelato was established at Lot 15,
1 Floor, Kampung Limau Manis, 15050 Kota Bharu, Kelantan.
For over 22 years we've perfected our Sweet Gelato recipe to make each Sweet Gelato ice
cream taste better than the last, taking your taste buds on a delicious journey.
